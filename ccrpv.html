The Collapsing (1945-II/115BC-IV to 2015-II/50BC-IV)[edit | edit source]

For lore/history for before (And during, for CCRPIV) this period, see the CCRPII wiki and the CCRPIV wiki. For information relating to the World Union, see World Union.

At the beginning of this period there was peace for a few years in the II world, after the destruction of OSMD; however, that was not to last. A few remaining members of OSMD deployed a special new Scourge-based weapon upon the World Union (A remnant of this weapon would later be found in Magnum Savannah and captured by the Tal'dorese Empire in the IV world for unknown reasons—however, it is believed that its appearance may be related to the Homaric Pact Nations Warplinks) This ignited a series of wars called The Scourge Wars, which was among the most deadly wars in the history of CCRPII. During this period, Occidentalis Minor was nearly destroyed, and many nations vanished. International relations fell apart, the World Union collapsed, then the Great Universal Empire and the Praenuntian Union, and finally even the remnants of Socii Mundial dissolved. OSMD was never seen again, likely turned into a few of millions of Scourgii wandering the lands. Most nations lost contact with all other nations, except those who were maritime powers. Omari Robusta was the sole nation unaffected by the Scourge.

Eventually, the world picked up the pieces and rebuilt. The GUE and PU reformed and were on good terms, if significantly weaker than before. Most nations remained independent from the unions, however, which limited their power. Nations also avoided Occidentalis Minor as they all believed it was nothing but a Scourgii-filled wasteland, when in fact several small and advanced nations, including the remnants of the State of Fictistan, continued operating there. Economic and technological progress was slow, and—except for Occidentalis Minor and Omari Robusta—they did not have much more advanced tech at the end of this period than before it.

At the very end of this period, the maritime power Ckaian Dani Ckotian, which controlled the Strait of Punicea, made contact with the State of Fictia.

The Expanding (2015-II/50BC-IV to 2035-II/30BC-IV)[edit | edit source]

For CCRPIV history during this period, see the CCRPIV wiki.

When the CDC made contact with the State of Fictia, it brought about an even known as The Expanding, a period where the rest of the world's limited technology was replaced with new technology from the State of Fictia and other Occidentalis Minor states. This mostly happened without bloodshed; however, old ruling dynasties of various countries (Whether or not they had any political power) that had hid in OMi disputed the existing rulers of their respective nations, with some disputes successful and others not (The only partially successful dispute took place in Humerland, which decided to keep both kings. Humerland has two kings to this day: see All the Kings of the Homaric Raj). The only exception to this was the War of Succession, a short war (2017-2019) between the government of the State of Fictia and the Confederacy of Fictian Holdings. The CoFH was afraid that the State of Fictia would force their government on the CoFH, and so declared war. In the process, the CoFH was accidentally conquered by the State of Fictia, and so the Fictian Empire was re-declared, and entered the GUE. In declassified government documents it was later revealed that the State of Fictia had no intention of conquering the Confederacy of Fictian Holdings; however, it is now widely considered good that the war happened, as it reinstated Fictia as a world power and accelerated the adoption of the more advanced technologies.

In 2020, a common virus (Specifically a coronavirus) from Fictia started a panic in many countries as a pandemic began. Many governments overreacted to the disease between 2020 and 2023; however, it was jokingly called the "beer plague" in Fictia as a reference to Corona (A popular alcoholic beverage from Canciona), and to refer to the ridiculous overreaction to the pandemic that policymakers effected as if they had drunk too much Corona. It is often called the "beer plague" across the World Union today.

In 2027, Omari Robusta ended its isolationist policy and declared war on the Fictian Empire due to Fictia attempting gunboat diplomacy with them. The war lasted 8 years, and was known as the War of the Final Supremacy. Due to the fact that Omari's technology was more advanced even than Fictia's, the war was long, hard, and destructive. Omari had the PU supporting them, while Fictia had the rapidly modernizing GUE on their side. However, two years before the end of the war (2033), the PU decided that terrible relations with the GUE and significant losses of life were too large of a cost for supporting Omari. After a long debate, the PU declared neutrality and began rebuilding relations with the GUE. In the end, Omari was forced to surrender; however, Fictia allowed them to retain sovereignty. At this point, Omari joined the world's nations, an event widely considered the end of The Expanding.

The Joining (2035-II/30BC-IV to 2135-II/70-IV)[edit | edit source]

For CCRPIV history during this period, see the CCRPIV wiki.

The joining was a long, 100-year period of developing space travel and gradual expansion of the superunions. There were no fewer wars than during The Collapsing or The Closing; however, most of them were not major wars. There were many wars between independent states, or between the GUE or PU and an independent state. There were no more supremacy wars.

In 2041 the first moon colony was founded as a joint project between the GUE and PU. A secret anti-union alliance was formed between several nations in 2042 and was called Libertas Mundial.

By 2047 half the world were members of either the GUE or PU.

Omari Robusta joined the Praenuntian Union in 2050. The People's Republic of Canciona came under increasing pressure from both unions—as well as from Libertas Mundial—to join them, but Mikhail Chornuff-Ranoluv did not back down. Libertas Ceased pressuring Canciona in 1952.

Mikhail Chornuff-Ranoluv died in 2054 and his son Vladimir Ranoluv III became dictator. The pressure from the unions had increased further, but Vladimir was leaning toward joining Libertas Mundial. In 2055 he secretly joined Libertas Mundial. By this point, all of Mediterrae, Alituria, and Borealis were GUE or PU. Orientalis Major was consolidating as well.

For another 40 years a cold war began between the GUE and PU, which were allied, and the remaining independent nations—many of which, but not all, were members of Libertas Mundial.
